Why We Are Building This

Traditional aftercare leaves a critical gap. Young adults leave structured programs and enter a vacuum with no daily support, no early warning system, and often, a loss of community.

During early customer interviews, we kept hearing pain points like: "I don't know if my kid is okay between therapy sessions," or "My teen gets a lot out of his AA group, but unfortunately they only meet once per week," or "We tried using existing digital platforms, but none of them provided a comprehensive companion for the recovery journey."

Engaged Recovery is our answer. We are building a platform, specifically for young adults, that builds recovery habits through daily exercises, allows coaches and clinicians to select exercises based each client's needs, and gives families visibility without creating a surveillance dynamic.
